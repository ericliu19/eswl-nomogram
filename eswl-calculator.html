Model details

A multivariable logistic regression model was fitted to predict successful stone fragmentation (defined as absence of clinically significant residual fragments) following ESWL. The model was developed on 343 consecutive procedures and internally validated using 1000-iteration bootstrap resampling.

Linearity of continuous predictors was confirmed using restricted cubic spline testing (ANOVA-based, total nonlinear p=0.78). No spline transformations were required.
